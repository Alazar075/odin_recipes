arrosto

Chicken Arrosto (properly called Pollo Arrosto in Italy) is a classic Italian rustic dish.
It features a whole chicken roasted with olive oil, garlic, and fresh herbs like rosemary and thyme, resulting in crisp skin and incredibly juicy meat.

ingridients

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oi
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • 1 lemon, cut in half

steps

  1. Dry and Prep the Chicken
  2. Stuff the Cavity
  3. Season the Skin
  4. Roast to Perfection
