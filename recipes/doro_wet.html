doro_wet

Doro Wot is a traditional Ethiopian spicy chicken stew, slow-cooked with onions, berbere spice, and clarified butter,
often served with hard-boiled eggs and injera, making it the national dish of Ethiopia.

  • agge
  • onions
  • enjera

  2. Dry-Sweat the Onions
